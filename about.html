About US

What our FOUNDER's say..

Greetings,

We are honored to introduce Ourselves "GV RAMA REDDY" & "G PAVAN REDDY"
as the Founders of SV EGGS Poultry. SV EGGS is more than just a business;
it is a labor of love that represents my deep-rooted Passion for sustainable
farming and providing thehighest quality eggs to our valued customers.

When we embarked on this journey, We envisioned a place where hens could flourish
in a pest free and nurturing environment.Our farm is designed to ensure the well-being
of our hens, allowing them to enjoy fresh air and sunlight, and indulge in a
nutritious diet. Happy hens lay the best eggs, and we take pride in the
exceptional quality that comes from their contented lives.

Our commitment to sustainability drives every aspect of our operation.
From environmentally conscious farming practices to responsible waste management,
we strive to minimize our ecological impact.It is our firm belief that by caring
for our planet, we are nurturing the future for generations to come.

At SV EGGS Poultry, we prioritize excellence in every egg we produce.
Our dedicated team of farmers and experts work tirelessly to maintain the
highest standards of hygiene, quality, and food safety. Each egg is a
testament to the meticulous care and attention we invest in our craft.

But SV EGGS is not just about eggs; it's about fostering individual who value
exceptional food and responsible farming. I invite you to visit our farm,
witness our practices firsthand, and experience the connection between our
hens and the incredible eggs they lay.

As the Founder's, We committed to fostering transparency and open communication
with our customers. Your feedback is valuable to us, and it drives us to continuously
improve and offer an even better experience.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to all our customers who have chosen SV EGGS
Poultry as their source of wholesome, delicious eggs.Your support allows us to
continue our mission of providing finest to your table.

Thank you for being a part of the SV EGGS family
